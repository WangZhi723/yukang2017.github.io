Publications

EAT-NAS: Elastic Architecture Transfer for Accelerating Large-scale Neural Architecture Search [arXiv]
Jiemin Fang*, Yukang Chen*, Xinbang Zhang, Qian Zhang, Chang Huang, Gaofeng Meng, Wenyu Liu, Xinggang Wang.

We propose Elastic Architecture Transfer for large-scale neural architecture search. Architectures are first searched on a small dataset, CIFAR-10, and the best is chosen as the basic architecture. Then, we accelerate the search process on the large-scale dataset, ImageNet, by using the basic architecture as initial seeds for evolution. We conduct the experiments on MNAS search space and find architectures competitive to MNASNet with much less computational resources.

Joint Neural Architecture Search and Quantization [arXiv]
Yukang Chen, Gaofeng Meng, Qian Zhang, Xinbang Zhang, Liangchen Song, Shiming Xiang, Chunhong Pan.

We propose to joint neural architecture search and quantization. 1) Our approach can provide existing networks with learning-based quantization policies. It can yield higher accuracies than the float models. 2) Two models are obtained with joint search of architectures and quantization policies: JASQNet and JASQNet-Small that achieves 2.97% error rate with 0.9 MB on CIFAR-10.

Reinforced Evolutionary Neural Architecture Search [arXiv]
Yukang Chen, Gaofeng Meng, Qian Zhang, Shiming Xiang, Chang Huang, Lisen Mu, Xinggang Wang.

We propose Reinforced Evolutionary Neural Architecture Search (RENAS) that integrates RL and EA in architecture search. We discover a powerful architecture with very limited computational resources, 4 GPUs across 1.5 days. It achieves a promising accuracy on the mobile setting Imagenet with limited parameters. It also outperforms MobileNet, MobileNet-v2 and NASNet in semantic segmentation on the PASCAL VOC.
